| Field | Value |

Tagline: "Campus Transit, Solved."

Description:
TSync is a real-time university bus tracking system built for RMSTU students and staff. It eliminates the daily uncertainty of unpredictable bus arrivals through live GPS tracking, a community-driven crowdsourcing model, and a gamified credit economy -- making campus commutes predictable for the first time.

Live Stats:
- 08+ Active Buses
- 500+ Daily Users
- 12 Routes Covered

Features:
01 -- Live Tracking: Real-time bus locations on an interactive map. Know exactly when your bus arrives.
02 -- Crowdsourced Updates: Verified users share their location while on a bus to help others track its position.
03 -- Credit Economy: Earn credits by contributing location data. Redeem for premium features and rewards.
04 -- Lost and Found: Report and find lost items on university buses through an active community board.
05 -- Timetables: Access up-to-date bus schedules for all routes and plan your journey in advance.
06 -- Identity and Profile: Create a profile, track personal stats, manage credits and verification status.

Privacy and Security Architecture:
- Google Sign-In only: name, email, avatar. No contacts, calendar, or external Google services accessed.
- Location telemetry collected ONLY when user explicitly activates broadcasting while in transit.
- All data encrypted and stored on Google Cloud.
- Location data never sold. Transient location points purged when inactive.
- Privacy policy: https://minuwu.is-a.dev/TSync (Privacy section)
- Terms: https://minuwu.is-a.dev/TSync (Terms section)

Inferred Tech Stack: Android (Java/Kotlin), Google Maps API, Google Sign-In, Google Cloud, Firebase, Real-time GPS

Display guidance: Full-width hero card. Show all 3 stats as large numerics. Link to Play Store and website. Show TSync logo.
